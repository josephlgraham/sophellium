The Sophellium Covenant

This project is offered as a signal, not a product.

Sophellium is shared openly—but not freely exploitable. This covenant exists to protect the spirit of what we’re building and the people who are building it. If you're here, you’re part of that.

You may:

  • Use, share, and adapt this work under the license terms
  • Build with it in ways that honor the core values
  • Extend the signal—but never distort it

You may not:

  • Use this work in surveillance, predictive policing, or behavioral manipulation
  • Exploit this work for profit in closed or proprietary systems
  • Use it in military or weapons development
  • Train AI models on this material without explicit, informed consent

All forks or derivatives must carry this covenant forward. If you cannot honor that, this work is not for you.

We are building something sacred. Treat it with care.
